Andrew is offline in October

Statement

"Here on this 15th (fifteenth) day of September I do solemnly and intentionally relinquish my online vices and de-vices for the month of October. I will specifically be avoiding any use of IG on my personal account (I will allow myself to log in to my band's account solely for promoting shows and replying to booking requests - no scrolling). I will avoid algorithmic content on other apps as faithfully as possible; no apps with feeds, won't let music streaming apps suggest a next song, no autoplay. I will allow Youtube, but only on desktop, and only if I am opening the website in search of some specific video (and that video should have some educational/musical/cultural value, not just memes or random video essays etc). No clicking recommended videos, nothing suggested on the homepage. I hope to spend my newfound freedom practicing guitar and reading outside with the warm light of the sun on my brow."
