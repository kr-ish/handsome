Nice November

Motivation

Why not?

Parameters

Perform one act of kindness every day. This could be for a stranger, an acquaintance, a loved one, or for yourself. Switch it up- sometimes a stranger, sometimes yourself, etc.. The opportunity to be nice will present itself to you everyday and all you have to do it be open to it.

Some ideas:

  • Buy flowers from a local florist and give them to someone.
    • If you're in New York- I really like Verdure Florist in Bedstuy, Stolen Magnolia in Bushwick/Ridgewood, and Sunny's Florist in the East Village.
  • Think of someone in your life you are really proud of and tell them.
  • And many more

As always, follow your heart.

Join

If you would like to participate, email me a statement. A picture of you would be nice too. The only hard requirements are your statement, and letting me know if you would like it added to the website.

At the end of the month, I will check in with each participant and request a reflection.
