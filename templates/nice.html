Nice November

Motivation

Why not?

Parameters

  • Perform one act of kindness each day.

It may be offered to a stranger, to an acquaintance, to a loved one, or to yourself. Vary the gesture: one day outward, another day inward. The chance to be kind will find you daily â€” your task is only to remain open to it.

As always, follow your heart.

Possibilities

  • Buy flowers from a local florist and give them to someone.
    • If you are in New York City- I really like Verdure Florist in Bedstuy, Stolen Magnolia in Bushwick/Ridgewood, and Sunny's Florist in the East Village.
  • Recall someone in your life of whom you are deeply proud, and tell them so.
  • Cook for someone.
  • Write someone a card.
  • Forgive someone.
  • And many more

Remember- someone can be you. Just do something nice!

Join
