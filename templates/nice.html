Motivation

Perform one act of kindness every day. Could be for a stranger, an acquaintance, a loved one, or for yourself. I would suggest trying to switch it up- sometimes a stranger, sometimes yourself, etc.. The opportunity to be nice will present itself to you everyday and all you have to do it be open to it.

As always, follow your heart.
