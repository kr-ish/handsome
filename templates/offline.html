Offline October

Motivation

Social media is expertly designed to steal your attention and prey on human addiction. Almost everything is an ad. If you are so privileged to do so, maybe a month long break from any and all of it would do good for your soul. Perhaps it would even inspire some new ideas or actualize some latent ambitions. Though the parameters I've outlined for myself below are a tad convoluted- yours could be as simple as deleting your most used infinitely scrolling app. Follow your heart!

All you have to do is pick something and stick to it.

Parameters

I will not use
  • TV and online video streaming (YouTube included)
  • Social Media (deactivated already but reinforcing this - Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn)
  • Dating apps (deleted already but reinforcing this)
  • Discord
  • Podcasts
  • Online adult content
Additionally
  • Grayscale phone screen.
  • Block all app notifications except ones that would really require immediate attention- messages, calls, pet sitting app.
I will allow
  • Live local radio
  • TV and video in the company of others
  • Audiobooks
  • Whatsapp, but no stories.
  • Duolingo
I will continue to
  • Send my friends and family photos and ask them to send me photos.
  • Go to to the movies
    • Dazzling images, on a huge silver screen. Sound that I can feel.
I will pick up
  • Daily music production practice - even just 5 minutes
