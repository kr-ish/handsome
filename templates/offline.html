Offline October

Motivation

Social media is expertly designed to steal your attention and prey on human addiction. Almost everything is an ad. If you are privileged enough to step away from any or all of it, for just a month — consider what this might restore to your soul. In that silence and space, ideas that had been waiting patiently might arrive; latent ambitions waiting to be actualized might finally form. The framework I have set for myself below may be a bit elaborate- yours need not be. It could be as simple as deleting the one app that sucks you into a hole with its infinite scroll. Follow your heart!

"Do everything with a mind that lets go. Don't accept praise or gain or anything else. If you let go a little you a will have a little peace; if you let go a lot you will have a lot of peace; if you let go completely you will have complete peace."
― Ajahn Chah

All you have to do is pick something and stick to it. LFG (let fucking go).

Objections

"I don't have a scrolling problem."
Try it anyway — completely block out one form of social media. It's just one month.
"I pay an app / device to limit my screen time."
See above.
"Where will I get recipes?"
Write some down ahead of time! Or pick up a cookbook that's been laying around, or a new one from the bookshop.
"But I have a highly curated algorithm!"
It is my view that God has left you (but you can always find Her again).
"You have attention deficit disorder and should be medicated."
Word.
